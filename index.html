Ryan's Music Taste

Hello. I am a senior in high school and I enjoy listening to music. On this page, you'll get to see the different kinds of music that define my taste and hopefully you'll see some cool reccomendations that you'll end up enjoying.

My favorite Artists

Kanye West

In my opinion, Kanye is the best musical artist of our generation. Every single album that Kanye puts out sounds different from his last, but none of them sacrafice quality for innovation. His albums 808s and Heartbreaks and The College Dropout are two of the most influential albums of all time, paving the way for many new artists such as Drake, Kid Cudi, and Travis Scott.

Michael Jackson

Michael Jackson is one of the most talented people to ever live. His music still remains timeless and his vocal abilites were imperssive even from a very young age. His album Thriller is a classic and his music is perfect for any mood.

Frank Ocean

Frank Ocean is my favorite RNB artist. His soothing voice paired with amazing production makes for some of the best sad songs I've ever heard. His second album, "Blonde" is one of the best things I've ever heard and especially hits at night.
